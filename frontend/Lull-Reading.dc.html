linger /ˈlɪŋɡər/ CET6 · 高频
v. 徘徊；逗留；（气味、感觉等）萦绕，挥之不去
英文释义
(v.) to stay in a place longer than necessary, typically because of a reluctance to leave; to be slow to disappear.
例句 EXAMPLES
The smell of coffee lingered in the kitchen.
咖啡的香味在厨房里久久不散。
She lingered at the door, unwilling to say goodbye.
她在门口徘徊，不愿道别。
